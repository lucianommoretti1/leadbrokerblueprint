Refund Policy

Effective: May 2026.

Plain English: if the course doesn't earn its keep, email within 30 days of purchase and we will refund you in full. No questions, no friction, no "let us interview you to keep your money." We'd rather refund you than have you regret it.

1. The 30-day refund window

You may request a full refund within thirty (30) days of your purchase date. The 30 days starts on the calendar day of your purchase. We do not extend the window automatically, but unusual circumstances (e.g., serious illness, family emergency) can be discussed individually.

2. How to request a refund

Email leadbrokerblueprint@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund request." Include:

  • The email address you used at purchase.
  • The approximate date of purchase.
  • (Optional) A sentence about what didn't work for you. Helpful feedback, not required.

We respond within two business days and process the refund within five business days of confirmation. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

3. What happens to your access after a refund

When we issue your refund, we revoke access to the members area and the Course materials. We ask that you delete any downloaded files. This is consistent with the license described in our Terms of Service.

4. Tier-specific notes

  • Blueprint ($297): standard 30-day refund applies. No proration; full refund.
  • Blueprint + Add Ons ($1,297): standard 30-day refund applies. If you have already attended a live Q&A or quarterly update call, the call's portion is not deducted; we refund in full.
  • Blueprint + Mentorship ($2,997): standard 30-day refund applies. If you have already used mentorship calls, refunds are prorated as follows: full refund within 30 days minus $250 per mentorship call already completed. If no mentorship calls have been used, full refund.

5. After 30 days

We do not offer refunds after 30 days as a matter of policy. If you have an exceptional situation, email us — we'll discuss it individually. We don't make blanket promises beyond 30 days, but we're reasonable people.

6. What we won't do

  • We won't make you "prove" you completed the course before refunding.
  • We won't subject you to a retention call or "win-back" sequence.
  • We won't deduct payment-processor fees from your refund (we absorb those).
  • We won't refuse a refund because you've already accessed the materials.

7. Chargebacks

If you'd like a refund, please email us first. We will resolve the request quickly. Chargebacks initiated through your card issuer without first contacting us cause unnecessary friction for both sides and may result in revocation of access while the chargeback is pending. We will not initiate counter-disputes for refunds requested in good faith within the 30-day window.

8. Why this policy exists

Our four marketing rules include a real refund policy. We mean it. A premium-priced course earns its price by delivering value; if it doesn't earn yours, the right answer is to give you your money back and learn from the feedback. We'd rather you tell other people the refund was easy than tell them we made it hard.

9. Contact
